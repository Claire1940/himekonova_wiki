<Section icon={Swords} title="Main Stat Priorities and Substat Goals">

Achieving the correct stat balance is critical for Himeko Nova. As a Fire-based damage dealer, she benefits from elemental damage bonuses and critical consistency. The **himeko nova prefarm** should aim for a 1:2 ratio between Crit Rate and Crit Damage, while ensuring her Speed is high enough to act before enemies in high-difficulty content.

| Relic Slot | Recommended Main Stat | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Ensures consistent damage output. |
| **Boots** | ATK% / Speed | Higher ATK for raw power or Speed for frequency. |
| **Sphere** | Fire DMG Boost | Direct multiplier for all her attacks. |
| **Link Rope** | Energy Regen Rate | Crucial for maintaining Ultimate uptime. |

For substats, the priority is straightforward but difficult to master. You should prioritize Crit Rate until you reach approximately 70%, then shift focus entirely to Crit Damage and Speed.

1. **Crit Rate**: Essential for triggering consistent crits on multi-hit abilities.
2. **Crit Damage**: Scales her damage ceiling exponentially.
3. **Speed**: Allows for more turns within the cycle limits of endgame content.
4. **ATK%**: Provides the base value for all damage calculations.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Trap">
Do not ignore Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR). While an ATK% rope looks better for sheet damage, an ERR rope allows Himeko Nova to use her Ultimate more frequently, resulting in higher total damage across a full combat encounter.
</Callout>
