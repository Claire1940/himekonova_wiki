<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Relic and Planar Ornament Farming">

When farming gear materials for Himeko Nova, your primary target is the **Navigator Is See Sees It** relic set. This set is custom-tailored for her playstyle, providing a massive 54% DMG boost to both Skills and Ultimates, alongside a permanent 12% ATK increase. Since her Assist Skills count as using Skills, maintaining maximum stacks on this set is incredibly easy.

Alternative sets like Wavestrider or Scholar can serve as temporary placeholders, but they fall significantly behind in overall damage output. If you are facing Quantum-weak enemies, a high-quality Quantum set can perform well, but it remains situational compared to the universal power of the Navigator set.

| Relic Set | 2-Piece Bonus | 4-Piece Bonus | Performance Rating |
|---|---|---|---|
| Navigator Is See Sees It | +12% ATK | +54% DMG on Skill/Ult | ★★★★★ (Best in Slot) |
| Wavestrider | +10% CRIT Rate | Conditional DMG Boost | ★★★☆☆ (Alternative) |
| Scholar | +12% CRIT Rate | Ultimate DMG Boost | ★★★☆☆ (Placeholder) |

For Planar Ornaments, the **Fallen Star** set is highly recommended. It grants an 8% CRIT Rate and a massive 32% CRIT DMG boost if both the wearer and a teammate belong to the Trailblaze Companion faction. If you run Dan PT in your team, Bananamusement offers an identical crit value distributed entirely into CRIT DMG. In permanent Decimation teams, Rutilant Arena becomes a competitive option due to the high CRIT DMG saturation of that state.

| Planar Set | Critical Bonuses | Condition | Synergy Level |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Team Composition and Synergies">

Himeko Nova requires at least one Trailblaze Companion on her team to function effectively at E0. This faction includes Remembrance Trailblazer (RMC), Dan Heng PT, Welt, Sunday, and March 7th Hunt. Depending on your roster, you can build a team focused on permanent Verdict, permanent Decimation, or a dual-state swap strategy.

| Team Archetype | Core Members | State Strategy | Combat Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Verdict Hyper | Sunday, Sparkle, Dan PT | Permanent Verdict | Single Target & Bosses |
| Decimation AoE | Blade, Welt, Huohuo | Permanent Decimation | Multi-Target & Waves |
| Dual State Swap | RMC, Welt, Himeko Nova | Dynamic Swapping | Variable Target Fights |

The Verdict state is ideal for single-target or low-target fights, boosting her DMG by up to 200%. The Decimation state excels in high-target encounters like Pure Fiction, boosting the team's CRIT DMG by up to 200%. Running a dual-state team allows you to start in Decimation to clear waves, then swap to Verdict before launching her Ultimate to maximize single-target burst.
