<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Stats & Speed Tuning">

Himeko Nova has very high stat ceilings. Because she gains an abundance of Crit Damage from her kit and teammates like Sunday or Sparkle, your primary focus on relics should be reaching the **100% Crit Rate** threshold. This ensures her multi-hit Ultimate never misses a critical strike, which is vital for her damage consistency.

| Stat | Recommended Target | Why it matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | 4,000+ | Primary scaling for all Assist Skill hits |
| **Crit Rate** | 95% - 100% | Ensures consistency on her 9-hit Ultimate |
| **Crit Damage** | 180%+ | Scaled further by Decimation mode |
| **Speed** | 134 or Base | Depends on your Action Advance support |

<Callout type="info" title="Speed Tuning Guide">
If playing with **Sparkle**, keep Himeko at base speed or 134. If playing with **Sunday or Bronya**, ensure Himeko is exactly **1 Speed higher** than the support (e.g., Himeko 135, Sunday 134) to maximize the "double turn" efficiency.
</Callout>
