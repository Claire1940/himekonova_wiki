<Section icon={Target} title="Trace Upgrade Requirements and Boss Drops">

Maxing out traces is where the majority of your stamina will be spent. Himeko Nova is a high-investment damage dealer who relies heavily on the multipliers found in her major ability nodes. Neglecting her traces will significantly hamper her performance in late-game content like the Forgotten Hall or Pure Fiction.

The trace materials follow a three-tier structure. You will need a massive amount of the highest tier, Cosmic Sand Pit, to reach level 10 on her primary abilities. Furthermore, her advanced nodes require rare weekly boss drops and Tracks of Destiny.

| Trace Tier | Material Name | Total Amount Needed | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tier 1 (Green) | Celestial Globe | 18 | Early Trace Levels |
| Tier 2 (Blue) | Galaxy Framework | 69 | Mid-Range Upgrades |
| Tier 3 (Purple) | Cosmic Sand Pit | 139 | Max Level Traces |
| Weekly Boss | Vanquished Flows Reticence | 9 | Advanced Ability Nodes |
| Special | Tracks of Destiny | 8 | Major Traces and Level 10s |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Tracks of Destiny are a limited resource. If you are low on these, prioritize her Ultimate and Talent first, as these contribute the most to her overall damage rotation.
</Callout>

The farming route for these materials typically involves repeated runs of the Crimson Calyx. Given the high quantity of Cosmic Sand Pit required, it is advisable to use any available "Double Drop" events specifically for these materials.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Main Stats and Substat Priorities">

To make the most of the **Himeko Nova ascension materials** you have farmed, you must ensure her stats are balanced. A common mistake is over-investing in Crit Damage while neglecting Crit Rate, leading to inconsistent performance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Crit Consistency">
    Equip a Crit Rate body piece. Aim for at least a 70% Crit Rate before focusing heavily on Crit Damage. Consistent crits are vital for her multi-hit abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Energy Flow">
    Use an Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) Link Rope. Himeko Nova's Ultimate provides a massive portion of her total DPS; maintaining high uptime is more valuable than a small Attack boost from an ATK rope.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Speed and Attack">
    Equip ATK boots unless you are hitting specific speed breakpoints for your team. Since she scales well with raw power, ATK boots are generally preferred in most 2026 meta builds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Relic Slot | Recommended Main Stat | Priority Substats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Body | Crit Rate / Crit Damage | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, SPD |
| Feet | ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, SPD, Break Effect |
| Sphere | Fire Damage Bonus | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, SPD |
| Link Rope | Energy Regeneration Rate | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, SPD |

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Goals">
For a high-end Himeko Nova build, aim for **3000+ ATK**, **75% Crit Rate**, and **160%+ Crit Damage**. Achieving these stats will allow her to clear endgame content with ease.
