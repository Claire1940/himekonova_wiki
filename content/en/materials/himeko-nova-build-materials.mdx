### Understanding Companion Protocols
When an ally uses an Assist Skill, Himeko Nova enters one of two states based on the character:
- **Verdict (Single Target Focus)**: Triggered by Sunday, Dan Heng, or Trailblazer. Increases Himeko's DMG by 125% and Ultimate DMG by 125%.
- **Decimation (AoE Focus)**: Triggered by Welt, March 7th, or Himeko (Base). Increases party CRIT DMG by 125% and Skill CRIT DMG by 125%.
