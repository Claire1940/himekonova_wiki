<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Stat Priorities and Optimization">

Choosing the right main stats on your relics is just as important as the set bonus itself. For Himeko Nova, balancing her Crit ratio is the highest priority. Because she relies on multi-hit abilities, a low Crit Rate can result in wildly inconsistent damage numbers.

| Relic Piece | Main Stat Priority | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | **Crit Rate** | Ensures consistent damage across multi-hits |
| **Boots** | **Attack%** | Maximizes raw scaling for Skill/Ult |
| **Sphere** | **Fire DMG Boost** | Essential multiplier for elemental DPS |
| **Link Rope** | **Energy Regen Rate** | Faster Ultimate rotations for higher DPS |

**Substat Priority:**
1. **Crit Rate**: Aim for at least 70% for consistency.
2. **Crit DMG**: Stack as high as possible once Crit Rate is stable.
3. **Speed**: Useful for reaching breakpoints (134+), though Attack% is often competitive.
4. **Attack%**: Provides a solid floor for all damage calculations.

<Callout type="info" title="Build Note">
While Speed is generally king in turn-based combat, Himeko Nova benefits so much from raw Attack that you should only swap to Speed boots if you can hit the 134 breakpoint without sacrificing too much Crit value.
</Callout>
