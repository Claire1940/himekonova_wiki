When Himeko Nova is active on the field, she deploys the Starblazer Territory. This summons the Starblazer, an independent entity that accumulates aura and grants all allies one Assist Skill usage. When an ally uses this Assist Skill, the Starblazer performs a bouncing attack that hits all enemies on the field. Because this action counts as Himeko using a Skill, it easily triggers relic bonuses and talent multipliers. On Himeko's own turn, using the Assist Skill does not consume any charges and acts as her primary action, dealing enhanced damage with an increased bounce count.

<Callout type="tip" title="Skill Rotation Tip">
Do not forget to use Himeko Nova's normal Skill once every three turns. This action grants her the Navigator buff, which automatically restores ally Assist Skill charges at the start of her turns while providing a steady stat boost to the entire team.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Verdict vs Decimation: Dual-State Mastery">

The core of Himeko Nova's combat loop lies in transitioning between two distinct states: Verdict and Decimation. These states are triggered when a specific Trailblaze Companion uses an Assist Skill on their turn. Choosing which state to prioritize depends entirely on the enemy lineup and your current team composition. 

| State | Trigger Companions | Primary Buffs | Free Assist Skill Condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Verdict** | Trailblazer, Dan Heng, Sunday | +100% DMG, +100% Ultimate DMG | Teammates cast Ultimates 2 times |
| **Decimation** | March 7th, Evernight, Welt, Himeko | +100% CRIT DMG, +100% Skill CRIT DMG | Allies hit enemies 9 times |

The Verdict state is tailored for single-target or low-target encounters. By pairing Himeko with Sunday or Dan Heng, she gains massive raw damage multipliers that amplify her Ultimate. On the other hand, the Decimation state is perfect for wave-based content like Pure Fiction. When triggered by characters like Welt or March 7th, the entire team receives a massive critical damage boost, allowing sub-DPS units to contribute significant damage.

<Callout type="warning" title="E0 Turn Economy Restriction">
At E0, only Trailblaze Companions receive an immediate extra turn after using an Assist Skill due to her A4 passive. If you use non-companion units to trigger the Assist Skill, you will lose valuable turn actions.
</Callout>
