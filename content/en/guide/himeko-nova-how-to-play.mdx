When Himeko Nova is active on the field, she deploys the Starblazer Territory, spawning a Starblazer construct. This construct grants all allies one Assist Skill usage. Pressing the Assist Skill button commands the Starblazer to unleash an AoE bounce attack. If Himeko launches this on her own turn, the attack does not consume an Assist Skill charge, effectively replacing her standard Skill action with a stronger variant.

The core of her gameplay revolves around entering one of two distinct combat states based on which ally activates the Assist Skill:

| Combat State | Trigger Allies | Primary Buffs | Extra Assist Skill Trigger Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | Trailblazer (MC), Dan Heng, Sunday | +100% DMG, +100% Ultimate DMG | Teammates launch Ultimates 2 times |
| **Decimation** | March 7th, Evernight, Welt, Base Himeko | +100% CRIT DMG, +100% Skill CRIT DMG | 9 enemies hit by allies or Himeko |

<Callout type="tip" title="State Management Tip">
To optimize her output, match her state to the encounter. Trigger Verdict during low-target or boss encounters, and swap to Decimation in wave-based modes like Pure Fiction.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Rotation & Combat Flow">

Executing Himeko Nova's rotation effectively ensures she maintains her state buffs while maximizing the frequency of her powerful Ultimate. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Technique Activation">
    Use her Technique before entering combat to trigger her Cruise state. This automatically executes her Skill at the start of each wave, immediately generating energy and preparing her Assist Skill charges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="State Triggering">
    Allow a fast Trailblaze Companion to take the first turn and use the Assist Skill. This action triggers her A4 talent, granting that companion an immediate extra turn while placing Himeko into either Verdict or Decimation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Skill and Charge Management">
    Use Himeko's normal Skill once every 3 turns to refresh her Navigator buff. On all other turns, use her Assist Skill. Ensure your teammates attack or use Ultimates to trigger the free extra Assist Skills granted by her active state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Execution">
    When her custom energy bar is full, unleash her Ultimate. Follow the optimal sequence: Annihilation Pulse (at 3 Source Energy), Particle Beam three times to rebuild energy, and repeat before the final automatic hit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimal Ultimate Sequence">
Thanks to her A6 major trace, Himeko Nova gains 3 Source Energy when casting her Ultimate. The standard sequence is: Pulse -> Beam -> Beam -> Beam -> Pulse -> Beam -> Beam -> Beam -> Pulse -> Final Hit.
</Callout>

</Section>

Himeko Nova requires at least one Trailblaze Companion in her squad to unlock her extra turns and activate her dual states. Do not build a team entirely of Trailblaze Companions unless you have specific Eidolons that support that setup.

The most viable Trailblaze Companions for her teams include:
- **Sunday**: Excellent action-forwarder and single-target buffer for Verdict teams.

- **Remembrance Trailblazer (RMC)**: Provides CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, and True DMG buffs.
- **Welt**: Ideal for Decimation teams, providing delays and slows.
