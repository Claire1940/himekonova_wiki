When Himeko Nova is on the field, she deploys the *Starblazer Visioscape* territory. This grants all allies one Assist Skill charge. Activating this Assist Skill counts as Himeko using her own Skill, triggering her relic buffs and dealing Fire damage to all enemies, followed by three bounce attacks. The behavior of this skill shifts dramatically depending on which faction member triggers it, splitting her gameplay into two distinct states.

| State | Trigger Faction | Core Buffs | Free Assist Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | MC, Dan Heng, Sunday | +100% Himeko DMG, +100% Ult DMG | Teammates use Ultimate 2 times |
| **Decimation** | March 7th, Welt, Evernight, Base Himeko | +100% Team CRIT DMG, +100% Skill CRIT DMG | Allies hit 9 enemy targets |

To maintain these states, Himeko must use her standard Skill once every three turns to refresh her *Navigator's Semaphore* buff, which increases all allies' damage by 25% and automatically recovers Assist Skill charges at the start of her turn.

<Callout type="info" title="Passive Trace Synergy">
Her A4 Trace ensures that when a Trailblaze Companion uses an Assist Skill, they immediately gain an extra turn. This allows you to weave Assist Skills into your rotation without sacrificing your supports' action economy.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Relic Builds, Planar Ornaments, and Stat Targets">

To support the high damage ceilings unlocked by her Eidolons, Himeko Nova requires specific relic configurations. Because she self-buffs her CRIT DMG and DMG% extensively through her talent and territory states, your primary goal is securing high CRIT Rate and Attack stats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Navigator Isee Sees It">
    This set is tailor-made for her. It grants a permanent 54% DMG bonus to her Skill and Ultimate, alongside 12% ATK. Since Assist Skills register as standard Skills, Himeko maintains maximum stacks effortlessly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Planar Ornaments">
    Run *Fallen Star* (8% CRIT Rate, 32% CRIT DMG when paired with another Trailblaze Companion). If running Dan PT, *Bananamusement* offers identical value. In permanent Decimation teams, *Rutilant Arena* can pull ahead due to high CRIT DMG saturation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Your Combat Stats">
    Aim for 100% CRIT Rate in combat. Because her kit and teammates provide massive CRIT DMG, a CRIT Rate chest piece is almost always mandatory unless you run heavy CRIT Rate buffers like Sunday.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Gear Piece | Main Stat Priority | Substat Priority | Target Threshold |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | CRIT Rate (or CRIT DMG with Sunday) | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK% | 90%+ CRIT Rate (Out of Combat) |
| **Boots** | ATK% (Slow Build) or SPD (134/135) | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, SPD | 3500+ ATK (ATK Boots setup) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Rope Selection Tip">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Top Team Compositions & Rotation Strategies">

Himeko Nova's team building relies on whether you want to focus on a single state (Verdict or Decimation) or run a dual-state swapping composition.

### Verdict Teams (Single-Target & Boss Fights)
Verdict focuses on hyper-carrying Himeko Nova's personal Ultimate and Assist damage. At E0, Sunday, Sparkle, and Dan Heng PT represent her strongest lineup. Sparkle pulls Himeko forward, Himeko acts and resets the Assist charge, and Sunday uses his Assist Skill to pull Himeko again. Dan Heng PT is mandatory here to prevent missing Assist Skill activations. At E2, you can swap Dan Heng PT for Huohuo, as any character can now trigger the extra turn.

### Decimation Teams (High-Target & Pure Fiction)
Decimation shines in AoE scenarios where you can consistently hit multiple targets. A sustainless lineup featuring RMC (Remembrance Trailblazer), Blade, and Welt offers incredible safety and amplification. Blade acts as a tanky wind-up target, Welt slows and delays enemies, and RMC provides massive CRIT and True DMG buffs.
