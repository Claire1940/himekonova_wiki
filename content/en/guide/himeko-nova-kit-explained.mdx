The Assist Skill is the heart of the **himeko nova kit explained** here. Normally, the team shares one use of this skill, which resets at the start of Himeko's turn. If Himeko uses the Assist Skill herself, she does not consume the charge, effectively replacing her standard Skill action. Crucially, the A4 Trace allows any **Trailblaze Companion** who uses an Assist Skill to gain an immediate extra turn, preventing any loss in action economy.

| Feature | Description | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starblazer Territory** | Field effect summoned by Himeko | Enables the Assist Skill button for the team |
| **Assist Skill** | AoE attack followed by 3 bounces | Counts as Himeko using a Skill for relic buffs |
| **A4 Trace** | Immediate turn for Trailblaze units | Allows for massive combo chains and energy gain |
| **Navigator Buff** | 3-turn buff from Himeko's Skill | Increases ally DMG and resets Assist charges |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Assist Management">
Always use your Assist Skill on a **Trailblaze Companion's** turn rather than a generic support. This triggers the immediate extra turn, allowing your support to buff Himeko and then immediately use the Starblazer for extra damage and energy.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Dual Combat States: Verdict vs. Decimation">

Himeko Nova's versatility comes from her ability to enter two distinct states based on which ally activates the Assist Skill. Choosing the right state is critical for clearing high-difficulty content like Pure Fiction or Memory of Chaos in 2026.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Verdict State">
    - **Trigger**: MC, Dan Heng, or Sunday.
    - **Buffs**: +100% Total DMG and +100% Ultimate DMG.
    - **Free Assists**: Launches a free Assist Skill after allies use 2 Ultimates.
    - **Best for**: Single-target bosses and elite enemies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Decimation State">
    - **Trigger**: March 7th, Evernight, Welt, or Base Himeko.
    - **Buffs**: +100% Crit DMG (Allies) and +100% Skill Crit DMG.
    - **Free Assists**: Launches a free Assist Skill after hitting 9 enemies.
    - **Best for**: Multi-target waves and Pure Fiction.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The **Verdict** state is generally preferred for hypercarry setups where Himeko's Ultimate provides the bulk of the team's damage. Conversely, **Decimation** excels in teams with sub-DPS units like Welt or Blade, as the massive Crit DMG buffs apply to the entire team. Note that Himeko can only trigger free Assist Skills twice per state activation, but this limit resets every time she uses her Ultimate.

| State | Primary Buff | Trigger Condition | Ideal Scenario |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | **Ultimate DMG** | MC / Dan Heng / Sunday | Boss Fights / Elites |
| **Decimation** | **Crit DMG** | Welt / March / Evernight | Pure Fiction / Mob Waves |

<Callout type="info" title="State Swapping">
In complex fights, you can run a "Dual State" team (e.g., Sunday and Welt). Start in Decimation to clear the first wave of mobs, then have Sunday trigger the Assist Skill to swap Himeko into Verdict before she unleashes her Ultimate on the boss.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Ultimate Rotation and Energy Management">

Himeko's Ultimate is a cinematic sequence where she beams the squad into the Starblazer. While it looks complex, the optimal rotation is fixed for 99% of combat scenarios. Understanding **Source Energy** is the key to maximizing her multipliers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Pulse">
    Activate the Ultimate to enter the Starblazer state. Start with an **Annihilation Pulse** to deal initial AoE damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Source Energy Accumulation">
    Use **Particle Beam** three times. Each beam builds one stack of Source Energy (up to a cap of 3 at E0).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enhanced Pulse">
    Once at 3 stacks, use **Annihilation Pulse** again. The A6 Trace provides a massive DMG multiplier increase when consuming max stacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Hit">
    After the sequence, Himeko automatically performs a Final Hit. If she kills an enemy, she can continue the damage chain, similar to Acheron's mechanics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Himeko uses standard Energy rather than a custom resource, but her high cost makes Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) ropes a viable niche choice if you lack her signature Light Cone. Her Technique is also vital; it auto-kills weak mobs and ensures she uses her Skill at the start of every wave, setting up her Assist charges immediately.

| Ability | Type | Energy Gain | Special Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill** | Buff/Reset | 30 | Resets Assist Skill & grants Navigator |
| **Particle Beam** | Ult Action | 5 | Builds Source Energy stacks |
| **Annihilation Pulse** | Ult Action | 10 | Consumes stacks for massive DMG |
| **Technique** | Field/Buff | N/A | Auto-Skill at wave start |

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Trap">
Avoid using Annihilation Pulse with 0 or 1 stack of Source Energy. The damage loss is significant. Always follow the 3-beam sequence unless the enemy is at 1% HP and you need to end the turn quickly.
</Callout>

</Section>
