<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Himeko Nova Skills and Rotations">

The core of effective **himeko nova gameplay** lies in the rhythmic execution of her skill set. Unlike characters who spam buttons, Himeko rewards players who understand the "weight" of her attacks. Her basic attack sequence consists of heavy strikes that build the "Nova Gauge," which can then be expended for enhanced fire-infused maneuvers.

Her Ultimate ability is the centerpiece of her kit, delivering a high-burst damage window that can incinerate entire waves of enemies. Timing this Ultimate with support buffs is critical for endgame performance.

### Skill Breakdown and Mechanics

| Skill Name | Type | Key Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blazing Cleave** | Basic ATK | 4-sequence greatsword strike, builds SP and Nova Gauge |
| **Nova Ignition** | Special/Combo | Consumes gauge for a spinning fire attack with gather effect |
| **Phoenix Rise** | Ultimate | Massive overhead strike dealing 2500%+ Fire DMG |

<Callout type="tip" title="Rotation Secret">
Always perform the full Basic Attack sequence before using your Special/Combo. The final hit of the sequence provides a significant boost to the next Fire DMG instance, maximizing your burst efficiency.
</Callout>

### Optimal Combat Rotation
To maximize damage output, follow this specific sequence during boss encounters:
1. **Support Setup**: Use your support characters to gather enemies and apply Fire Vulnerability.
2. **Gauge Building**: Execute two full cycles of **Blazing Cleave** to fill the Nova Gauge.
3. **Ignition Phase**: Activate **Nova Ignition** to shred enemy fire shields.
4. **The Finisher**: Once support buffs are active, unleash the **Phoenix Rise** Ultimate for maximum burst.
