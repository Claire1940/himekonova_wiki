<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Team Compositions and Speed Tuning">

The "Engine of Destruction" team is currently the most popular way to play the **himeko nova full kit**. This setup utilizes Sparkle and Sunday to manipulate the turn order, allowing Himeko Nova to act multiple times per cycle and spam her Assist Skills through her allies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Setup">
    Equip **Sparkle** with 160+ Speed and **Sunday** with slightly less (e.g., 158). This ensures Sparkle acts first to pull Himeko Nova forward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Loop">
    Use **Dan Heng's** turn to trigger an Assist Skill. Then, use Sparkle to pull Himeko Nova. Himeko Nova uses her skill to refresh her Assist charges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sunday's Intervention">
    Sunday acts immediately after Sparkle. Before Sunday uses his skill on Himeko Nova, trigger her Assist Skill on Sunday's turn to maximize energy gain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ultimate Finish">
    Once Himeko Nova reaches 150 energy, unleash her Ultimate. In the **Verdict** state, this will likely clear the entire wave instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy Bonus">
Using **Astral Express** members is not just for lore; it triggers Himeko Nova's second passive, which grants the ally an extra turn after they use her Assist Skill. This effectively doubles the action economy of your supports.
</Callout>

</Section>
