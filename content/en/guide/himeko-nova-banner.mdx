<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Team Compositions and Assist Looping">

The true power of the **himeko nova banner** is realized when she is paired with other members of the Astral Express. Her passive abilities grant extra turns and energy when Express Crew members use her Assist Skill.

**The "Infinite Loop" Team:**
- **Main DPS**: Himeko Nova
- **Support 1**: Sunday (Speed tuned to 161+)
- **Support 2**: Sparkle (Speed tuned to 163+)
- **Support 3/Sub-DPS**: Dan Heng or Trailblazer (Preservation/Harmony)

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup">
    Use Himeko Nova's Technique to enter battle, creating the "Platform" and granting an immediate Assist Skill charge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sparkle Advancement">
    Sparkle uses her Skill to pull Himeko Nova forward. Himeko uses her Skill to buff the team and refresh the Assist charge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sunday Loop">
    On Sunday's turn, use the Assist Skill button first. Because he is an Express member, he gets an extra turn. Then, use Sunday's Skill to pull Himeko up again.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Eradication">
    Once Himeko Nova reaches 150 Energy, unleash her Ultimate. This resets the "Verdict" or "Decimation" charges, allowing the loop to continue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="The Assist Button">
Never forget to press the Assist Skill button on your ally's turn! Forgetting this button can result in a loss of over 500,000 damage per rotation in endgame content like Memory of Chaos.
</Callout>
