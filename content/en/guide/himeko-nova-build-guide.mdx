<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Mechanics and Playstyle">

When optimizing your characters, utilizing an expert **himeko nova build guide** is essential to unlocking her dual-mode potential. Himeko Nova introduces a highly dynamic playstyle centered on spamming Ultimates and utilizing the unique Assist Skill mechanic. When she is active on the field, she deploys the Starblazer Territory, summoning the Starblazer to farm aura. This grants all allies one Assist Skill usage per Himeko turn. Activating this skill commands the Starblazer to perform an AoE attack followed by three bounce attacks, counting as a Skill activation for relic triggers.

The Assist Skill changes properties based on which ally triggers it. If a Trailblaze Companion (such as Remembrance Trailblazer, Dan Heng PT, or Sunday) activates the skill, Himeko Nova enters the **Verdict** state. This increases her overall damage by 100% and her Ultimate damage by an additional 100%. Alternatively, if March 7th, Evernight, Welt, or base Himeko activates the skill, she enters the **Decimation** state. This increases all allies' CRIT DMG by 100%, with an extra 100% CRIT DMG buff applied specifically to Skills and Assist Skills. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Skill Frequency Tip">
Do not forget to use Himeko Nova's normal Skill once every three turns. Using her normal Skill grants her the Navigator buff for three turns, which automatically resets your allies' Assist Skill charges at the start of each of her turns.
</Callout>

At E0, when a Trailblaze Companion activates the Assist Skill, they receive an immediate extra turn due to her A4 Trace. This prevents you from losing valuable actions during your setup phases. Her Technique allows her to enter a Cruise state, instantly defeating minor overworld enemies and automatically casting her Skill at the start of combat waves to secure early energy and immediate Assist Skill charges.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Ultimate Rotation and Eidolon Breakdown">

Himeko Nova utilizes a custom energy bar to execute her Ultimate. When activated, she beams her entire squad into the Starblazer, allowing her to fire Particle Beams to build Source Energy (up to 3 stacks) and unleash Annihilation Pulses to consume those stacks. Thanks to her A6 Trace, she automatically gains 3 Source Energy upon casting her Ultimate, simplifying her execution.

The standard manual rotation for her Ultimate is highly consistent:
1. **Annihilation Pulse** (Consumes the initial 3 stacks)
2. **Particle Beam** three times (Rebuilds 3 stacks)
3. **Annihilation Pulse** (Consumes the rebuilt stacks)
4. **Particle Beam** three times (Rebuilds 3 stacks)
5. **Annihilation Pulse** (Consumes the final stacks)
6. **Automatic Final Hit** (Unleashes massive combined multipliers)

<Callout type="warning" title="Eidolon vs. Light Cone Priority">
If you are deciding between pulling for her E1 or her S1 Light Cone, prioritize her **S1 Light Cone** first due to the massive damage bump and energy comfort. However, if you have the resources, **E2** is her strongest early stopping point, completely eclipsing S1.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="Teammate Gear Optimization">

### 1. Single-Target Verdict Team (Hypercarry)
This team is designed to vaporize single targets and bosses by maintaining permanent uptime on her Verdict state.
- **Main DPS**: Himeko Nova (Slow setup, ATK% Boots)
- **Support 1**: Sunday (Provides action-forwarding and massive damage buffs)
- **Support 2**: Sparkle (Pulls Himeko Nova forward, manages skill points)
- **Trailblaze Enabler**: Dan Heng PT (Triggers Verdict state, acts as sub-DPS)

### 2. Sustainless Decimation Team
Perfect for high-difficulty content where you can lock down enemies through speed debuffs and crowd control.
- **Main DPS**: Himeko Nova (Base Speed)
- **Sustain/Tank**: Blade (Taunts enemies, absorbs damage, triggers follow-ups)
- **Debuffer**: Welt (Delays enemies, applies slows, triggers Decimation)
- **Buffer**: Remembrance Trailblazer (Summons Mem to absorb hits, buffs CRIT stats)
