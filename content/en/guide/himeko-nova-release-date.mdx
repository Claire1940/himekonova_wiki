<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Verdict vs. Decimation: Dual Combat States">

The core of Himeko Nova's tactical flexibility lies in her two distinct combat states: **Verdict** and **Decimation**. These states are triggered depending on which "Trailblaze Companion" uses their Assist Skill. This allows players to adapt their strategy mid-battle to handle boss encounters or large waves of enemies.

| State | Primary Focus | Buff Effect | Best Used In |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | Single-Target / Bosses | Increases Himeko's Ultimate DMG by 100% | Memory of Chaos / Boss Fights |
| **Decimation** | Multi-Target / Farming | Increases Team CRIT DMG and Skill CRIT DMG | Pure Fiction / Calyx Farming |

<Callout type="info" title="State Activation">
To enter the **Verdict** state, use an Assist Skill with characters like **Sunday** or **Dan Heng**. For **Decimation**, use the Assist Skill with characters like **Welt** or **March 7th**.
</Callout>

</Section>
