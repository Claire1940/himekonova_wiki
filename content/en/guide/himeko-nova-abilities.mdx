The core of her kit is the Assist Skill. When Himeko or a Trailblaze Companion triggers it, she enters either **Verdict** (increased DMG) or **Decimation** (increased CRIT DMG). Maintaining these states is the key to her high-end performance.

| State | Primary Buff | Trigger Condition | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | +100% DMG | Trailblaze Companion Skill | Single Target / Bosses |
| **Decimation** | +100% CRIT DMG | March/Welt/Base Himeko Skill | Pure Fiction / AoE |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Note">
Always ensure Himeko uses her Skill every 3 turns to refresh the "Navigator's Semaphore" buff, which provides essential team-wide DMG increases.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Rotation and Team Synergy">

Team composition is critical for Himeko Nova. You need units that can trigger her Assist Skill without sacrificing their own turn economy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup">
    Use Technique to enter the "Cruise" state, ensuring Himeko starts the wave with an immediate Skill usage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="State Activation">
    Deploy a Trailblaze Companion to activate the primary state (Verdict or Decimation) early in the rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assist Skill Spam">
    Cycle through allies to trigger Assist Skills, resetting the count with Himeko's own turns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Burst">
    Execute the Pulse-Beam-Pulse sequence when Source Energy is maximized for optimal damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>
