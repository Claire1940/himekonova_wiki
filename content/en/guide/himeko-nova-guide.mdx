Himeko Nova introduces a revolutionary combat system to the game through her **Starblazer Territory**. Unlike traditional damage dealers, her kit revolves around the **Assist Skill**, a brand-new ability type that allows allies to command the Starblazer to attack. <YouTubeEmbed videoId="MZgIr1TSIyw" title="The BEST Guide to Himeko Nova! | Best Builds, Relics, Teams" />

The core of Himeko Nova's gameplay is her Skill, which grants all allies one **Assist Skill** usage. When an ally uses this button, Starblazer performs an AoE attack followed by three bounce hits. Crucially, if a **Trailblaze Companion** uses the Assist Skill, they receive an immediate extra turn, effectively making the Assist Skill a free action that batteries Himeko's unique energy resources.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Mechanic Tip">
Himeko Nova does not consume an Assist Skill usage on her own turn. Instead, her Assist Skill replaces her standard Skill action, dealing increased damage and higher bounce counts without depleting the team's shared usage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Verdict vs. Decimation: Strategic Mode Selection">

Himeko Nova functions in two distinct states, determined by which Trailblaze Companion activates the Assist Skill. Choosing the right state for the current encounter is the difference between a struggling run and a 0-cycle clear.

| State | Activation Units | Primary Buffs | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | Dan PT, MC, Sunday | +100% DMG, +100% Ult DMG | Single-target bosses, low enemy counts |
| **Decimation** | Welt, March, Evernight | +100% Crit DMG, +100% Skill Crit DMG | Pure Fiction, high-density waves |

<Callout type="warning" title="Rotation Warning">
You can only trigger two free Assist Skills per state activation. This count resets every time Himeko Nova uses her Ultimate. Ensure you consume your free charges before casting her Ult to avoid wasting damage.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Team Synergy: Trailblaze Companion Archetypes">

Building the right team is the most critical part of any **himeko nova guide**. You must balance the need for Trailblaze Companions with the necessity of powerful Harmony supports.

| Team Archetype | Slot 1 (DPS) | Slot 2 (Support) | Slot 3 (Sub-DPS/Support) | Slot 4 (Sustain) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hyper Verdict** | Himeko Nova | Sunday | Sparkle | Dan PT |
| **Sustainless AoE** | Himeko Nova | RMC | Welt | Blade |
| **F2P Friendly** | Himeko Nova | RMC | March 7th (Hunt) | Huohuo |

### The Sunday/Sparkle Core
This is Himeko's highest damage ceiling. **Sunday** and **Sparkle** provide constant action advancement. By using **Dan PT** in the fourth slot, you ensure that every rotation triggers the **Verdict** state, giving Himeko the massive Ultimate multipliers she needs to delete bosses.

### The Welt/Blade Sustainless Core
In this setup, you focus on the **Decimation** state. **Welt** provides slows and delays, while **Blade** acts as a tanky sub-DPS. This team relies on the sheer volume of attacks to trigger free Assist Skills constantly.

<Callout type="tip" title="Dual State Strategy">
In fights where the number of enemies changes (e.g., Bosses that summon adds), you can swap states. Start in **Decimation** to clear the adds quickly, then switch to **Verdict** using a Dan PT Assist Skill before Himeko Ults to finish the boss.
</Callout>
