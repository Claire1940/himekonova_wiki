<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Mechanics & Starblazer Synergy">

Himeko Nova is not just a visual upgrade; her kit introduces the **Starblazer Visioscape** territory. This mechanic changes how the entire team interacts with the turn order. When Himeko Nova is on the field, she summons the Starblazer, which acts as an independent entity that responds to ally actions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deploy Territory">
    Use Himeko Nova's Talent to immediately deploy "Starblazer Visioscape" at the start of the battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Generate Source Energy">
    Use her Skill or have allies use Assist Skills to generate "Source Energy," which is capped at 3 (or 6 at E6).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Assist Skills">
    Allies can use their unique Assist Skill to call upon Starblazer for a Fire-type follow-up attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Ultimate">
    Consume all Source Energy to launch "Orbital Annihilation Pulse," dealing massive AoE Fire DMG.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The **himeko nova voice actor** for Starblazer, Nick Olson, recorded specific lines for these assist triggers. Every time an ally like March 7th or Dan Heng uses an assist, the Starblazer responds with a "Support attack beckoned" or "Navigator confirmed" line.

| Ability Name | Type | Key Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Enkindle the First Lodestar** | Basic ATK | Deals 110% ATK as Fire DMG |
| **Navigator's Semaphore** | Skill | Recovers Assist uses, boosts ally DMG |
| **Orbital Annihilation Pulse** | Ultimate | Consumes Source Energy for AoE/Bounce DMG |
| **Of Fire and Far Faring** | Talent | Summons Starblazer, grants RES PEN |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
