<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Himeko Nova and the Assist Mechanic">

Himeko Nova introduces a highly unique playstyle to the tactical RPG landscape in 2026. As an Erudition character, her core kit revolves around the innovative Assist Skill mechanic. This allows her to deal massive damage out of turn, drawing power directly from her teammates. Rather than acting as a traditional selfish carry, her performance scales exponentially based on the synergy and actions of her fellow Astral Express crew members.

To master her gameplay, players must understand her two distinct team-wide buffs: Verdict and Decimation. Verdict activates when pairing her with Dan Heng, Sunday, or the Trailblazer, offering a massive 100% damage boost. Decimation triggers via multi-hit attacks from characters like Welt or March 7th, shifting the focus toward team-wide Critical Damage.

Every time Himeko Nova uses her skill, she grants a 20% damage boost to the entire team. More importantly, this action refreshes her Assist Skill charge at the start of every turn. By managing these charges and coordinating with Express crewmates, you can trigger her Assist Skill on almost every single ally turn.

<Callout type="info" title="Verdict Priority">
Prioritize Verdict teams over Decimation teams. The 100% Ultimate damage bonus from Verdict provides a much higher damage floor and ceiling for general content.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Relics, Planar Ornaments, and Stat Benchmarks">

Building Himeko Nova is relatively straightforward, as she shares many stat priorities with traditional Erudition damage dealers.
