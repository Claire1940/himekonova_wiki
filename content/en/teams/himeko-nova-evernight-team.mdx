<Section icon={Target} title="Himeko Nova Evernight Team Rotation Steps">

Executing the correct rotation is the difference between clearing a stage with seconds to spare and failing the timer. The **himeko nova evernight team** relies on a strict sequence of skill activations to stack all available multipliers before Himeko Nova unleashes her Ultimate. Follow these precise steps during combat to maximize your damage output:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Support Setup">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="QTE Trigger">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Himeko Entry">
    Trigger Himeko Nova's QTE. Immediately use her weapon active to enter the enhanced heat state, increasing her attack speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Burst Ultimate">
    Unleash Himeko Nova's Ultimate within the support buff window. Follow up with charged attacks to consume remaining heat stacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
