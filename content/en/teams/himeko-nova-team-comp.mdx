Himeko Nova introduces a revolutionary playstyle to the Erudition path, focusing on her unique **Starblazer Territory**. Unlike traditional damage dealers who rely solely on their own turn actions, Himeko Nova thrives on the participation of her teammates through the **Assist Skill** mechanic. When Himeko is on the field, she deploys the Starblazer, which grants all allies usage of this special ability. Mastering the **himeko nova team comp** starts with understanding how these Assist Skills interact with her two distinct combat states: Verdict and Decimation.

The Assist Skill acts as an AoE attack followed by three bounce hits. If Himeko uses it on her turn, it doesn't consume a charge and gains increased multipliers. However, the true power lies in her **A4 Trace**, which grants an immediate extra turn to any **Trailblaze Companion** who uses the Assist Skill. This effectively allows your supports or sub-DPS units to act twice, generating massive energy and resetting Himeko's internal cooldowns.

| Ability Type | Energy Gain | Target Type | Special Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic ATK** | 20 | Single | Standard damage |
| **Assist Skill** | 30 | AoE + Bounce | Triggers State Buffs/Extra Turns |
| **Ultimate** | 5 | AoE | Enters Starblazer mode for burst |
| **Technique** | N/A | Support | Auto-Skills at start of every wave |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always use Himeko Nova's Technique before entering combat. It triggers her Skill automatically at the start of each wave, ensuring your Assist Skill charges are refreshed immediately for your fastest Trailblaze Companion.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Verdict vs. Decimation: Choosing Your State">

Himeko Nova's performance is dictated by which state she enters. This depends entirely on which Trailblaze Companion activates the Assist Skill first. This choice is the foundation of any successful **himeko nova team comp**.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Verdict State">
    - **Trigger**: MC, Dan Heng (all forms), or Sunday.
    - **Focus**: Massive Ultimate and overall DMG multipliers.
    - **Best For**: Single-target bosses or elite enemies.
    - **Buffs**: 100% DMG dealt and 100% Ultimate DMG.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Decimation State">
    - **Trigger**: March 7th, Welt, or base Himeko.
    - **Focus**: Critical Damage and Skill-based AoE.
    - **Best For**: Pure Fiction or high-density mob waves.
    - **Buffs**: 100% CRIT DMG for all allies.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The Verdict state is often preferred for high-difficulty content like Memory of Chaos. By running **Sunday** or **Dan Heng PT**, Himeko gains a staggering 200% total DMG increase for her Ultimate. Conversely, Decimation is the king of AoE. When paired with **Welt** or **Blade** (in sustainless setups), the team-wide CRIT DMG buff allows Himeko to clear waves with minimal effort.

| State | Primary Buff | Reset Condition | Best Ally |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Verdict** | **+200% DMG** | 2 Ally Ultimates | Sunday, Dan Heng PT |
| **Decimation** | **+200% CRIT DMG** | 9 Enemies Hit | Welt, March 7th |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
You can only maintain one state at a time at E0. If you accidentally trigger Decimation with Welt when you intended to use Verdict with Sunday, you will miss out on the massive Ultimate DMG multipliers required for boss phases.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Top Tier Himeko Nova Team Compositions">

Building the perfect **himeko nova team comp** requires balancing Skill Point consumption with the need for Trailblaze Companions. While Himeko herself is relatively SP-neutral or positive (using Assist Skills instead of normal Skills), her best supports can be hungry for resources.

### 1. The Hyper-Carry Verdict Comp
This is the gold standard for 2026. It utilizes the double-advancement synergy of Sunday and Sparkle to give Himeko Nova constant actions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Trigger">
    **Dan Heng PT** uses the Assist Skill to trigger the **Verdict** state and gains an extra turn to use his own Skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Support Buffing">
    **Sparkle** uses her Skill on Himeko Nova. **Sunday** uses his Skill to further boost Himeko's DMG and Energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Himeko Burst">
    Himeko Nova uses her Assist Skill (free on her turn) and follows up with her **Ultimate** once fully charged.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rotation Reset">
    Allies use Ultimates to trigger Himeko's free Assist Skills, refreshing her Verdict state and maintaining high uptime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. The Sustainless Decimation Comp
For advanced players, running a sustainless team with **Welt** and **Blade** provides unparalleled offensive pressure. Welt's slows and delays act as a pseudo-sustain, while Blade's follow-up attacks help reach the 9-hit requirement for Decimation resets.

| Team Member | Role | Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| **Himeko Nova** | Main DPS | Primary damage source through Assist/Ult |
| **Welt** | Sub-DPS/Sustain | Delays enemies, triggers Decimation |
| **Blade** | Sub-DPS/Tank | High hit count, draws aggro |
| **RMC** | Support | Grants True DMG and CRIT buffs |

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Strategy">
In the RMC/Blade/Welt comp, Himeko Nova can run base SPD. Since RMC and Welt provide significant delay and buffs, Himeko can focus entirely on ATK% boots to maximize her multiplier scaling.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Relic and Light Cone Optimization">

To make your **himeko nova team comp** truly shine, you must invest in the correct gear. Himeko Nova has very specific requirements due to her high innate CRIT DMG buffs and lack of innate CRIT Rate.

**Best Relic Sets:**
- **Navigator (4-Pc)**: The undisputed best-in-slot. It provides 54% DMG for Skills and Ultimates, which covers Himeko's entire kit.
- **Wavestrider (2-Pc) / Scholar (2-Pc)**: A viable transitional set if you haven't farmed a perfect Navigator set yet.

**Planar Ornaments:**
- **Fallen Star**: Specifically designed for Trailblaze Companion teams. It offers 8% CRIT Rate and 32% CRIT DMG.
- **Bananamusement**: A great alternative if you already have high CRIT Rate from sub-stats, as it focuses heavily on CRIT DMG.

| Stat | Recommended Value | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **ATK** | 3500 - 4000+ | High |
| **CRIT Rate** | 80% - 100% (In-Combat) | Critical |
| **CRIT DMG** | 150%+ | Medium |
| **SPD** | Base (103) or 134 | Team Dependent |

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Tip">
Because Himeko Nova gains massive CRIT DMG from her Verdict/Decimation states and teammates like Sunday, you should almost always use a **CRIT Rate chest** piece to ensure consistent damage.
</Callout>

**Light Cone Rankings:**
1. **Sailing the Astral (S1)**: Her signature cone. 32% DEF Ignore and 60% DMG for Assist/Ult. It is significantly ahead of other options.
2. **The Herta's Cone**: A strong F2P alternative from the Amphoreus shop.
3. **Night on the Milky Way**: Excellent for Pure Fiction but falls off in single-target boss fights.

</Section>
