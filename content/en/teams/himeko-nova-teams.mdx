To make **himeko nova teams** function at peak efficiency, you must adhere to specific speed breakpoints. The goal is to ensure your supports act in a sequence that allows Himeko to refresh her "Assist Skill" usage before the next ally takes their turn.

  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Sunday Sequence">
    Sunday should be tuned slightly slower than Sparkle (e.g., Sparkle 166, Sunday 163). After Himeko acts, Sunday uses the Assist Skill button, then uses his own skill to pull Himeko forward again.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Dan Heng Integration">
    Dan Heng acts in the gaps. Because he is an Express member, using the Assist Skill on his turn triggers Himeko's passive, granting him an extra turn and further accelerating Himeko's energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Burst">
    Once Himeko reaches 150 energy, unleash the Ultimate. This resets her Assist Skill charges and enters the Gundam state, where her "Source Energy" allows for repeated high-damage laser slashes.
