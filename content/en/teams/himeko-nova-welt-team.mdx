<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Rotation">

Executing the perfect rotation with the **himeko nova welt team** requires careful management of your Skill Points and Ultimate timings. Because you are running Welt as a utility support, you must balance his SP consumption with Himeko Nova's offensive needs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Debuff Application">
    Begin the battle by using Welt's Technique to delay enemy actions. On his first turn, use Welt's Skill to apply the Slow status effect to the primary targets, reducing their speed and building immediate energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Blade Stance Activation">
    Activate Blade's Skill to enter his enhanced state. Because this state lasts for three turns and only consumes one SP initially, Blade will generate charges for Himeko Nova through his basic attacks without draining your SP pool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decimation Mode Initiation">
    Use Himeko Nova's Skill on grouped enemies. This action, combined with Welt's slows and Blade's attacks, will rapidly push your charge counter toward nine. Once reached, Himeko Nova will automatically launch her free assist skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Loop Execution">
    Chain Welt's Ultimate immediately after an enemy turn begins to trigger Imprisonment. This maximizes the delay duration, allowing Himeko Nova and Blade to take consecutive turns and trigger another recursive assist loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Managing the Skill Point economy is the most critical aspect of this rotation. If Welt consumes too many points, Himeko Nova will be forced to use basic attacks, severely slowing down her charge generation.
