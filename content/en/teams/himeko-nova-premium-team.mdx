Himeko Nova introduces a revolutionary dual-mode playstyle centered on the **Starblazer Territory**. Unlike traditional DPS units, her primary damage output comes from the Assist Skill, a unique button that allows teammates to command the Starblazer to attack. In a **himeko nova premium team**, managing these Assist Skill uses is the difference between a mediocre run and a 0-cycle clear.

When Himeko Nova is on the field, she grants all allies one Assist Skill usage per Himeko turn. Using this skill with a **Trailblaze Companion** (MC, Dan Heng, Sunday, March, Welt) is mandatory, as it grants them an immediate extra turn, effectively making the Assist Skill a "free" action that builds energy and triggers Himeko's special states.

<Section icon={Target} title="Verdict vs. Decimation: State Management">

Maximizing your **himeko nova premium team** requires understanding when to switch between her two combat states. These states are determined by which Trailblaze Companion activates the Assist Skill first in a rotation.

**Verdict** is activated by MC, Dan Heng, or Sunday. It is designed for boss killing, providing raw DMG multipliers. **Decimation** is activated by March, Welt, or base Himeko, focusing on Crit Damage and multi-target hits. In a dual-state composition, you might start in Decimation to clear waves of adds, then swap to Verdict before Himeko unleashes her Ultimate on the boss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial State Activation">
    Use a Trailblaze Companion's Assist Skill immediately at the start of the wave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stacking Source Energy">
    Use Particle Beams during Himeko's Ultimate to reach 3 stacks of Source Energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Annihilation Pulse">
    Consume stacks with Annihilation Pulse for the A6 DMG multiplier increase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cycle Reset">
    Use Himeko's Skill every 3 turns to refresh the Navigator buff and Assist charges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Tip">
If you are running an E2 Himeko Nova, any character can trigger the extra turn. This allows you to bring powerful non-Trailblaze units like Silver Wolf or Jiaoqiu without losing momentum.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Builds: Relics, Ornaments, and Light Cones">

For a **himeko nova premium team**, Himeko's personal stats are heavily skewed toward Crit Damage through her self-buffs. This makes **Crit Rate** the most valuable substat to hunt for on your gear.

The **Navigator** set is non-negotiable for high-level play. It provides a 54% DMG boost to both Skills and Ultimates. Since Assist Skills count as Skills, Himeko keeps these stacks active permanently. For Planar Ornaments, **Fallen Star** provides the highest ceiling, offering 8% Crit Rate and 32% Crit Damage if you have two Trailblaze Companions on the team.
