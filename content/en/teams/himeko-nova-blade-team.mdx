The core synergy of a **himeko nova blade team** revolves around Himeko Nova's unique assist skill mechanics and Blade's robust self-sustaining sub-DPS capabilities. Unlike standard hypercarry configurations, Himeko Nova relies heavily on her allies to trigger her specialized combat states: Verdict Mode and Decimation Mode.

Blade fits exceptionally well into this team due to his low skill point consumption and frequent ultimate usage. In Verdict Mode, every ultimate cast by the team adds a charge to Himeko Nova. Once two charges are accumulated, she unleashes a free assist attack. Blade's ability to cycle his ultimate rapidly, combined with general buffers like Trinity, ensures a constant stream of free assist triggers.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Gear and Stat Priorities for Himeko Nova">

To maximize the damage output of your team, Himeko Nova must be geared with relics that complement her direct attack scaling. Unlike recent HP-scaling or break-focused characters, she thrives on classical damage dealer attributes such as Attack, Crit Rate, and Crit Damage.

The premier choice for her cavern relics is the four-piece S Navigator set. This set directly boosts her damage based on her navigation states and semaphore buffs. Pair this with the two-piece Fallen Star planar ornaments to enhance her ultimate and follow-up assist attacks.

| Relic Set | Pieces | Primary Bonus | Recommended Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| S Navigator | 4-Piece | Boosts attack and increases damage scaling during combat states | Cavern of Corrosion |
| Fallen Star | 2-Piece | Enhances ultimate damage and follow-up assist attack efficiency | Simulated Universe |

For main stats, look for Crit Rate or Crit Damage on the body piece, and Speed or Attack percent on the boots. The planar sphere should always feature Fire Damage, while the link rope can alternate between Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) or Attack percent depending on your team's energy generation capabilities.

| Gear Slot | Main Stat | Preferred Sub-stats |
|---|---|---|
| Body | Crit Rate / Crit Damage | Speed, Attack %, Flat Attack |
| Boots | Speed / Attack % | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Attack % |
| Planar Sphere | Fire Damage | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Speed |
| Link Rope | Energy Regeneration / Attack % | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Speed |

<Callout type="warning" title="Gear Warning">
While an Attack percent rope provides higher screenshot damage, an Energy Regeneration Rate rope is highly recommended if your team lacks consistent energy buffers. Faster ultimate cycling leads to more frequent orbital mech deployments, which yields higher overall damage in multi-cycle fights.
</Callout>
