<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Rotation Guide">

To maximize the damage output of the **himeko nova sunday team**, you must follow a strict rotation to ensure the Verdict state never drops. Sunday's ability to advance Himeko's turn while keeping her buffed is the secret to clearing high-difficulty content in 2026.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Technique Initialization">
    Use Himeko Nova's Technique before entering combat. This automatically triggers her Skill at the start of the wave, setting her Navigator stacks and resetting Assist Skill charges for the team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sunday Assist Start">
    On Sunday's first turn, use the **Assist Skill** button. This triggers the Verdict state for Himeko and grants Sunday an immediate extra turn due to his Trailblaze Companion status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sunday Skill Buff">
    Use Sunday's normal Skill to target Himeko Nova. This advances her action and provides her with massive Crit Rate and DMG buffs, allowing her to act immediately under the Verdict state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Sequence">
    Once Himeko's custom energy bar is full, trigger her Ultimate. Follow the **Pulse -> Beam x3 -> Pulse** sequence. Sunday's Verdict buff will apply to the entire duration of this multi-hit Ultimate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Assist Skill Timing">
Do not use Assist Skills on non-Trailblaze characters (like Sparkle or Huohuo) unless Himeko is at E2. Doing so at E0 will consume the charge without granting an extra turn, effectively wasting a character's action.
</Callout>

</Section>
