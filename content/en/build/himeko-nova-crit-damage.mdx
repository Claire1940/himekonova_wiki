When a Trailblaze Companion triggers her Assist Skill, Himeko Nova enters one of two distinct states: Verdict or Decimation. Entering the Decimation state—triggered by March, Evernight, Welt, or base Himeko—grants all allies a flat 100% CRIT DMG boost, alongside an additional 100% CRIT DMG boost to all Skill-based attacks (including Assist Skills). This massive influx of stats heavily shifts her optimal gear requirements, making critical stat balancing a top priority.

| State | Trigger Companions | Primary Buffs | Best Content |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verdict** | MC, Dan Heng, Sunday | +100% DMG, +100% Ultimate DMG | Single-target, bosses |
| **Decimation** | March, Evernight, Welt, Himeko | +100% to +200% CRIT DMG | AoE, Pure Fiction |

<Callout type="tip" title="State Management Tip">
To optimize your damage output, start your rotation in the Decimation state to accumulate easy Assist Skill charges in AoE scenarios, then swap to the Verdict state right before casting Himeko Nova's Ultimate to benefit from the massive Ultimate DMG multiplier.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Relics and Planar Ornaments">

Choosing the correct relic and planar ornament sets is the most straightforward way to stabilize your critical ratios. Because Himeko Nova self-buffs her critical damage extensively, her relic sets must provide generic damage multipliers and attack percentages to avoid stat dilution.

The **Navigator Isee Sees It** 4-piece set is her undisputed best-in-slot option. It provides a permanent 12% ATK increase and up to 54% DMG amplification on all Skills and Ultimates. Because Assist Skills count as using a standard Skill, Himeko Nova maintains maximum stacks of this buff with 100% uptime. Alternative options like Wavestrider or Scholar can serve as temporary placeholders but fall significantly behind in overall damage output.

| Relic / Planar Set | 2-Piece Bonus | 4-Piece / Set Bonus | Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Navigator Isee Sees It** | +12% ATK | +54% DMG on Skills & Ultimates | ★★★★★ |
| **Wavestrider** | +10% DMG | Conditional Ult DMG boost | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Fallen Star (Planar)** | +8% CRIT Rate | +32% CRIT DMG with Trailblaze Companion | ★★★★★ |
| **Bananamusement (Planar)** | +8% CRIT Rate | +36% CRIT DMG | ★★★★☆ |
| **Rutilant Arena (Planar)** | +8% CRIT Rate | +20% Basic/Skill DMG at 70% CRIT Rate | ★★★★☆ |

For Planar Ornaments, the **Fallen Star** set is highly recommended. It offers a permanent 8% CRIT Rate and an additional 32% CRIT DMG if both the wearer and at least one teammate belong to the Trailblaze Companion faction. If you are not running a compatible companion, **Bananamusement** provides a nearly identical critical value without the faction restriction.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Dilution Warning">
In permanent Decimation teams, the massive 200% CRIT DMG buff can cause your critical damage stats to dilute. In these specific setups, planar sets that offer generic DMG% multipliers, such as Rutilant Arena, can occasionally outperform pure critical damage sets.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Benchmarks and Gear Setup">

Because Himeko Nova receives up to 200% bonus critical damage from her kit and teammates, running a CRIT DMG chest piece often leads to diminishing returns and inconsistent damage. To ensure every hit lands critically, a CRIT Rate chest piece is almost always mandatory.

Your speed requirements are highly dependent on your team composition. If you run her alongside action-advancing supports like Sunday or Sparkle, you should run her at base speed (slow build) and focus entirely on offensive stats. If you run her without an action advancer, aiming for the 134 or 135 speed breakpoint is recommended to ensure she maintains her rotation cycles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Chest Piece">
    Equip a CRIT Rate chest piece to guarantee consistent crits. Only switch to a CRIT DMG chest if you have exceptional substats and a dedicated CRIT Rate buffer like Remembrance Trailblazer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose your Boots">
    Opt for ATK% boots if running with Sunday or Sparkle. Switch to Speed boots if running in traditional double-DPS or slow-utility setups.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Sphere and Link Rope">
    Equip an ATK% or DMG% Sphere. For the Link Rope, ATK% is standard, but an Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) rope is highly viable at S0 to alleviate her high energy costs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Substats">
    Prioritize reaching 90% to 100% in-combat CRIT Rate first. Once achieved, dump all remaining substat rolls into ATK% and CRIT DMG.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Gear Slot | Primary Stat | Desired Substats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | CRIT Rate | CRIT DMG, ATK%, Speed |
| **Feet** | ATK% / Speed | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK% |
| **Sphere** | ATK% / DMG% | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, Speed |
| **Rope** | ATK% / Energy Regen | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK% |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Stat Goals">
Aim for the following out-of-combat stats to ensure maximum performance: **3500+ ATK**, **80% CRIT Rate** (which easily pushes to 100% via in-combat buffs), and **130% to 150% CRIT DMG**.
</Callout>
