<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Leveling Priority for Himeko Nova Traces">

Upgrading your **himeko nova traces** in the correct order ensures you do not waste precious trace materials on low-impact abilities. Her kit relies heavily on a synergistic rotation between her Ultimate, passive Talent buffs, and Assist Skills.

| Ability | Priority | Key Function | Upgrade Recommendation |

### Ultimate & Talent: The Core Damage Engine
Himeko Nova's Ultimate costs 150 energy but deals devastating damage. It features a unique mechanic where she pilots the Starblazer, executing sequential attacks that culminate in a massive finisher. Her Talent is equally critical, providing a permanent field effect that grants her 20% RES PEN, 80% Crit DMG, and the ability to ignore weakness types on enemy toughness bars.

### Skill: The Setup Tool
Her Skill applies a 3-turn Navigator buff, which boosts all allies' damage by 20% and refreshes one Assist Skill use at the start of her turn. While crucial for rotation upkeep, upgrading the Skill primarily scales the minor utility buffs rather than her main damage output.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management">
Since Himeko Nova relies heavily on her Ultimate and passive Talent buffs for the bulk of her damage, do not waste early resources on her Basic Attack. Keep it at level 1 to save materials.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Verdict and Decimation Combat Modes">

Himeko Nova features two distinct playstyles depending on the faction of the teammate who utilizes her Assist Skill. Understanding how to cycle these modes is key to maximizing her trace-based passive multipliers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deploy Domain">
    Himeko Nova automatically expands her domain upon entering combat, summoning Starblazer and granting the team one Assist Skill charge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Ally">
    Position either a Verdict enabler (Sunday, RMC, DHPT) or a Decimation enabler (Welt, March 7th) in your team layout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Mode">
    Have the designated Express ally use the special Assist Skill on their turn to activate the corresponding mode and grant Himeko Nova massive buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cycle and Unleash">
    Use Himeko Nova's Ultimate to refresh assist charges and unleash maximum burst damage while maintaining the active mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The choice between Verdict and Decimation modes shifts her focus between hypercarry damage and team-wide dual-DPS synergy.

| Feature | Verdict Mode | Decimation Mode |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Playstyle Focus | Hypercarry DPS | Dual DPS / AoE Shield Break |
| Key Enablers | Sunday, RMC, Dan Heng, Trailblazer | Welt, March 7th, OG Himeko |
| Himeko Buffs | +100% Damage (+200% for Ultimate) | +100% Crit DMG to all allies |
| Assist Buffs | Free assist every 2 teammate Ultimates | Free assist every 9 enemy hits |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mode Overlap Caution">
Be careful when running a dual-mode team. Himeko Nova swaps modes every time a corresponding character uses the Assist Skill, which can override your desired buffs. Plan your action order carefully.
</Callout>

To unlock the full potential of Himeko Nova's traces, you must construct a team that facilitates frequent Assist Skill usage and provides strong action-advance utility.

This composition focuses entirely on funneling buffs into Himeko Nova, utilizing Sunday or Robin/RMC to advance her actions and maximize her Ultimate uptime.

- **Main DPS**: Himeko Nova
