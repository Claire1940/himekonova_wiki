<Section icon={Target} title="Best Stats and Substat Priorities">

Building Himeko Nova requires a departure from standard damage dealer configurations. While most characters demand high Speed to act frequently, Himeko Nova's damage is heavily concentrated in her off-turn assist skills. When allies use their turns to execute assist attacks, they trigger Himeko Nova's talent, generating energy and dealing massive coordinated damage. Consequently, building Speed boots on Himeko Nova is generally a significant damage loss.

Instead, you should focus entirely on maximizing her raw offensive scaling. Her body relic should almost always feature Crit Rate as the main stat. Because her talent grants her a massive 100% Crit Damage boost when assist skills are triggered, running a Crit Damage body piece often results in highly unbalanced stats and diminishing returns. Aim to reach at least a 75% Crit Rate threshold before heavily investing in external Crit Damage sources.

For her feet and planar sphere, Attack percentage is the dominant stat. While a Fire Damage sphere is highly competitive and should be used if it possesses superior substats, an Attack percentage sphere performs nearly identically due to the high damage multipliers built directly into her mecha abilities. Her Link Rope should always utilize Attack percentage, as her energy regeneration needs are easily met by her talent's passive energy generation.

| Relic Slot | Main Stat | Substat Priority | Build Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Body | Crit Rate | Crit Damage, ATK%, Speed | Kit provides high base Crit Damage |
| Boots | ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, ATK% | Speed boots are a significant DPS loss |
| Sphere | Fire DMG% / ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, ATK% | Choose the slot with superior substats |
| Rope | ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Speed | Energy Regeneration is not required |

<Callout type="warning" title="Speed Tuning Trap">
Do not chase Speed substats unless you are actively aiming to hit the 134 Speed breakpoint for specific cycle clears. For general gameplay, keeping Himeko Nova at base Speed while loading her with raw Attack and Critical stats yields far more consistent damage output.
</Callout>
