<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Team Rotations and Turn Order">

The effectiveness of **himeko nova speed** is realized through precise team rotations. Since Trailblaze Companions grant her the Verdict or Decimation states, their positioning in the Action Bar is paramount.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Wave Setup">
    Use Himeko Nova's Technique to auto-cast her Skill at the start of the wave. This generates immediate energy and sets her Assist Skill count to 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Companion Activation">
    A fast Trailblaze Companion (like Sunday or Dan PT at 160 SPD) acts first. They use the Assist Skill button, triggering Himeko's state and gaining an extra turn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="State Stacking">
    Depending on the state (Verdict needs Ults, Decimation needs hits), the team performs actions to generate free Assist Skill uses for Himeko before her actual turn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Himeko's Turn">
    Himeko acts, using her Assist Skill (which doesn't consume a charge on her turn) to deal massive damage and reset the ally usage count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Dual State Strategy">
In complex fights, you can bring both a Verdict ally (Dan PT) and a Decimation ally (Welt). Start in Decimation for AoE clearing, then swap to Verdict right before Himeko's Ultimate to maximize single-target nuke damage.
</Callout>

</Section>
