### Relic Set Comparisons

The Navigator set stands far ahead of the competition. It provides a massive boost to both Skill and Ultimate damage, which covers almost her entire damage profile. While alternative sets like Wavestrider or Scholar can be used as transitional pieces, they fall significantly behind in overall damage output.

| Relic Set | 2-Piece Bonus | 4-Piece Bonus | Performance Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Navigator in Sight** | +12% ATK | +54% Skill/Ult DMG | Best (★★★★★) |
| **Wavestrider** | +12% ATK | +40% Ult DMG (conditional) | Good (★★★☆☆) |
| **Quantum Set** | +10% Quantum DMG | Ignores 10-20% DEF | Situational (★★★☆☆) |
| **Scholar of Chivalry** | +12% Crit Rate | +20% Skill/Ult DMG | Transition (★★☆☆☆) |

### Planar Ornament Options

For Planar Ornaments, the choice depends heavily on your team composition. If you run at least one Trailblaze Companion alongside her, Fallen Star is the optimal choice. It offers an incredibly high Crit Value that matches Bananamusement but distributes the stats more favorably.

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Stat Priorities and Goals">

To maximize the performance of your **himeko nova relics**, you must focus on balancing her stats. Because she receives massive Crit DMG self-buffs from her kit (up to 80% passively, and an additional 100% in Decimation state), she suffers from heavy Crit DMG saturation. Therefore, prioritizing Crit Rate on your body piece is almost always the correct choice to ensure consistent damage.

### Recommended Main Stats

| Gear Piece | Main Stat | Preferred Substats | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Crit DMG, Crit Rate, ATK%, SPD | High |
| **Boots** | ATK% / SPD (134/135) | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK% | High |
| **Planar Sphere** | Fire DMG% / ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, SPD | Medium |
| **Link Rope** | ATK% / Energy Regen | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK% | Medium |

### End-Game Stat Targets

Aiming for these specific thresholds in 2026 will ensure your Himeko Nova can clear high-difficulty content like Pure Fiction and Memory of Chaos with ease:

- **Attack**: 3,500 - 4,000+ (highly achievable with ATK% boots and rope)
- **Crit Rate**: 80% - 90% out of combat (aim for 100% in-combat with ally buffs)
- **Crit DMG**: 130% - 150% from substats (her kit will push this past 250% in combat)
- **Speed**: Base SPD (with action forwarders like Sunday/Sparkle) or 134/135 SPD (for manual setups)

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Running a Crit DMG body piece without a dedicated Crit Rate buffer like Remembrance Trailblazer (RMC) or Sunday will result in highly inconsistent damage due to her lack of innate Crit Rate.
</Callout>
