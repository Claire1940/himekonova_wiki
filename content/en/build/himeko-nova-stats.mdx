<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal himeko nova stats and Stat Priority">

Achieving the perfect **himeko nova stats** requires a balance between her massive self-buffs and her base requirements. Since Himeko Nova provides herself with up to 200% Crit Damage depending on the active state, your primary focus on gear should be **Crit Rate** and **Attack %**. Speed is generally a secondary concern if you are running her with "Action Advance" supports like Sunday or Sparkle.

| Stat Type | Recommended Value | Priority Level | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crit Rate** | 80% - 90% (Out of combat) | **High** | Needs to hit 100% in-combat to ensure Ultimate hits crit. |
| **Attack** | 3,500 - 4,000+ | **High** | Her high multipliers scale aggressively with flat ATK stats. |
| **Crit Damage** | 130% - 150% (Base) | **Medium** | Lower priority due to massive 100-200% in-game buffs. |
| **Speed** | 103 (Base) or 135 | **Low** | Only build speed if not using Sunday or Sparkle advance. |
| **Energy Regen** | 100% - 119.4% | **Medium** | S1 or an Energy Rope helps alleviate her high Ult cost. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Crit Rate Threshold">
Do not neglect Crit Rate. Because Himeko Nova's Ultimate consists of multiple hits, failing to crit on the final "Annihilation Pulse" results in a massive DPS loss that no amount of Crit Damage can compensate for.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Team Synergy and Rotation Guide">

Building a team for Himeko Nova requires at least one **Trailblaze Companion**. These characters are the "enablers" that allow her to enter her empowered states. At E0, only Trailblaze Companions get an extra turn after using an Assist Skill, making them the most efficient users of the mechanic.

**Essential Team Roles:**
- **The Enabler**: Sunday, Dan Heng PT, or RMC (Triggers Verdict/Decimation).
- **The Buffer**: Sparkle, Robin, or Tribbie (Increases Action Value or Damage).
- **The Sustain/Sub-DPS**: Huohuo (Energy), Blade (Dual DPS), or Welt (Delay).

| Team Archetype | Members | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hyper Verdict** | Sunday, Sparkle, Dan Heng PT | Maximum Single Target Burst |
| **Dual State** | RMC, Welt, Blade | Versatility in mixed waves |
| **Sustainless** | RMC, Welt, Tribbie | High-end speed clearing |
| **Standard F2P** | Dan Heng PT, March 7th, Tingyun | Accessible progression |
