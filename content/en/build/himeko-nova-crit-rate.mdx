<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Himeko Nova Crit Rate Targets">

Achieving the correct **himeko nova crit rate** is critical because her ultimate and assist skills consist of multiple hits. If your Crit Rate is too low, individual hits of her bounce attacks will fail to crit, dragging down her overall damage output. You should always aim for **100% Crit Rate in combat**. 

Fortunately, reaching this benchmark is highly achievable due to her natural trace nodes and the powerful buffs provided by modern support characters. Himeko Nova receives 12% Crit Rate directly from her trace leveling tree. When building her, you must calculate both your out-of-combat stats (visible on the character sheet) and your in-combat stats (which include active support buffs).

| Stat Source | Crit Rate Granted | Condition |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Base Stats | 5.0% | Default for all characters |
| Minor Traces | 12.0% | Fully unlocked trace tree |
| Fallen Star (2-Piece) | 8.0% | Planar Ornament set passive |
| Crit Rate Body Piece | 32.4% | Main stat at Level 15 |
| Support Buffs (Sunday/RMC) | 10.0% - 20.0% | Active during combat turns |

If you run Himeko Nova alongside top-tier supports like Sunday or Ruin Map Creator (RMC), they will provide a persistent 10% to 20% Crit Rate buff during battle. Under these ideal team conditions, your out-of-combat stat screen only needs to display **80% to 90% Crit Rate**. Any higher will result in wasted stats that overcap past 100% in battle.

<Callout type="warning" title="Overcapping Warning">
Always calculate your active team buffs before finalizing your relic substats. Overcapping past 100% Crit Rate in combat wastes valuable substat rolls that could otherwise go into Attack% or Crit Damage.
</Callout>
